web-based data collection tools
Don't know how many unique records will need to be logged every time you enter data? Perhaps a dynamic form is the thing for you.

The Project Safe Flight Integrated Survey Tool (shown below) was created for NYC Audubon for just this reason.
Field researchers used to have to file reports one at a time - now they can log anywhere from 1 to 162 reports all at once, making data collection much easier and less error-prone (and maybe even fun?).

Take it for a spin - don't worry, it's just a demo version, you won't mess up their research.
