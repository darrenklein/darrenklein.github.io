Welcome to field-set.
There's not much to see here at the moment, but pretty soon this place will be all fancy.

The goal of field-set is to offer researchers, students, and non-profits a snazzy and personalized way to record, store, and access data for their projects.

For the time being, check out D-Bird.org or PSFIST to get a sense of what I'm talking about.

Even though the homepage isn't set up yet, field-set is ready to start cranking out projects.
